3 day plan made for campus gym

Workouts that fit between classes.

All you need is dumbbells, a bench, and a cable or two. Warm up 5-7 minutes, then follow the sets/reps below. Rest 60-90 seconds between sets.

Equipment checklist
  • Dumbbells
  • Bench
  • Cable or bands
  • Mat

Weekly flow

Day Focus Time goal
Day 1 Full Body Beginner 35-45 mins
Day 2 Lower Body + Core 30-40 mins
Day 3 Upper Body Push/Pull 35-45 mins

Full Body Beginner Workout

Five movements you can repeat weekly. Choose a weight you can control with good form.

  • Goblet squat — 3 sets × 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell bench press — 3 × 8-10
  • Single-arm dumbbell row — 3 × 10 each side
  • DB Romanian deadlift — 3 × 12
  • Plank — 3 × 30-40 seconds

Lower Body Day

Prioritize hips, quads, and glutes; finish with core.

  • Bodyweight or goblet squat — 3 × 12-15
  • Walking lunges — 3 × 10 steps each leg
  • Hip thrust or glute bridge — 3 × 12
  • Split squat — 3 × 8 each leg
  • Side plank — 3 × 20-30 seconds each

Upper Body Day

Push, pull, and shoulder stability.

  • Incline dumbbell press — 3 × 8-10
  • Lat pulldown or assisted pull-up — 3 × 8-12
  • Seated shoulder press — 3 × 10
  • Face pull or band pull-apart — 3 × 12-15
  • Hammer curl + triceps rope superset — 3 × 12 each

Progression tips

Add a little load

When you can hit the top of the rep range with clean form, bump weight by 5 lbs or add one extra set.

Track in notes

Log weight and reps in your phone. Seeing numbers go up keeps motivation high.

Warm-up in 5

Do light cardio for 3 minutes, then 2 sets of the first movement with lighter weight.
