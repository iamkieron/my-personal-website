How to make the most of STAYFIT

Keep it simple

Pick one workout track and stick to it for two weeks. Repeat weights, then add 5 lbs when it feels manageable.

Fuel like a student

Base meals on cafeteria staples: eggs, chicken, rice, beans, frozen veggies. Add fruit and water every day.

Make a cue

Attach gym time to an existing routine: “After class ends, I walk at the rec center for 35 minutes.”
