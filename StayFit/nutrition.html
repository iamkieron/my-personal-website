Perfect Meals To Get Yoked!!!

Simple meals that match your workouts.

You don’t need a chef’s kitchen. Grab easy protein, pair with carbs, and hydrate so you feel steady through class and training.

Do Not Cheat It!
  • Protein in every meal
  • Fruit daily
  • Water + electrolytes
  • 80/20 balance

Pre-workout snacks

Fast carbs & A little protein

  • Banana with peanut butter
  • Greek yogurt + berries
  • Granola bar + string cheese
  • Rice cake with honey

Campus coffee shop picks (Nash Library)

  • Oat latte + banana
  • Protein shake + pretzels
  • Bagel half + cream cheese

Post-workout meals

Cafeteria combo

Build a plate: chicken or tofu + rice/pasta + veggies. Add fruit on the side.

Dorm-friendly

  • Tuna packet + microwave rice + salsa
  • Rotisserie chicken wraps with spinach
  • Microwave oats with protein powder and berries

Snacky but balanced

  • Trail mix + Greek yogurt
  • Cottage cheese + fruit + granola
  • Chocolate milk + banana

Hydration tips

Baseline

Carry a 24 oz bottle to class. Finish two bottles by mid-afternoon, one more after the gym.

Electrolytes

Add a pinch of salt or a low-sugar electrolyte packet once a day if you sweat a lot.

Caffeine timing

Stop caffeine 6 hours before bed so recovery stays solid.
